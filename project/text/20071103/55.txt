wal mart takes stand on lawyers fees wal mart stores inc is trying to apply its pricing approach to law firms no increase in fees except for exceptional performers in a nov memo sent to law firms that represent the company a wal mart lawyer announced a "moratorium on across the board rate increases " the memo continues "until further notice we will only consider reasonable individual requests for rate increases for those attorneys in your firm who are performing at an exceptional level " corporate law firms which typically bill clients by the hour often implement annual across the board rate increases for firm attorneys wal mart gave firms a dec deadline to request rate increases for individual lawyers the memo reflects a sentiment among companies that "starting salaries for associates are too high " says daniel dilucchio jr a principal at altman weil inc
