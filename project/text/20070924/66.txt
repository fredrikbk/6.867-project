politics &amp economics china puts controls on tv talent shows associated press hong kong china has issued strict rules for television talent shows banning "american idol" style mass audience voting by mobile text messaging and the internet and forcing the programs out of prime time the move comes ahead of next months communist party congress the order dated friday acknowledged the value of tv talent shows but issued stringent requirements for the timing programming and judging of such shows the state agency said it wants "scientific judging standards " with voting permitted only among live audiences to avoid rigged voting by individuals using multiple mobile phone cards analysts have said authorities also feared the voting might encourage chinese to vent grievances on social issues
