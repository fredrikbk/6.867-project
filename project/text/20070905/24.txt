china backs nyse to open in china beijing chinas securities regulator approved nyse euronext the operator of the new york stock exchange to be the first foreign stock exchange to open a representative office in the country the approval fulfills part of a pledge made by china at the first meeting of the u s china strategic economic dialogue in december last year vice premier wu yi agreed to let nyse euronext and nasdaq stock market inc open such offices in mainland china the nyse will open its office in beijing china regulators said nyse euronext also will be required to submit regular reports about its operations
