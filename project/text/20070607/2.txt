acquisition chief leaves pentagon associated press washington the pentagons acquisition chief resigned wednesday the latest in a recent string of high level departures from the department kenneth krieg the defense undersecretary for acquisition technology and logistics said his resignation would be effective july or when a replacement was confirmed by the senate whichever came first mr krieg has been head of the departments research development and purchasing of new weapons systems aircraft and other technologies and a key adviser to the defense secretary on the militarys future programs mr krieg took on his job as undersecretary in june he said he was leaving to spend more time with his family he rose through the ranks under defense secretary donald rumsfeld who resigned under fire in november the chief of intelligence stephen cambone stepped down soon afterward
