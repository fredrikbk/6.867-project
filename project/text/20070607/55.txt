mitsui fudosan co real estate firm mulls takeover defense plan mitsui fudosan co japans biggest real estate firm said it is considering adopting a takeover defense plan highlighting concerns in corporate japan about becoming a target of a buyout fund the company is looking at various options and would seek the approval of its shareholders for any antitakeover plan it sought to adopt said a mitsui fudosan spokesman the spokesman said the company has many assets such as land and buildings that might be attractive to potential buyers "we are preparing for various possible cases " he said an increasing number of investment funds have been stepping up activity in japan in the hope of unlocking unrealized value in many japanese companies
