nasdaq is cleared to open china office shanghai nasdaq stock market inc said it has chinese government approval to open a representative office in beijing the news follows u s government efforts to pry open the countrys financial markets and comes weeks after nyse group inc said it had obtained similar rights the new office will serve marketing functions with local companies and put nasdaq officials in closer touch with chinese regulators according to a statement from the exchange china agreed to allow u s exchanges to open representative offices after consultations this year with treasury secretary henry paulson one measure of a broader effort by washington to give wall street a bigger role as china develops its capital markets
