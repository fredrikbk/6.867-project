christian in faith islamic in inclination "venice and the islamic world " the metropolitan museum of art march to july or www metmuseum org venice is a visually enchanting city the facades of the small buildings that line the citys winding streets surprise the eye at every turn with intricate sculptures many portraying prominent christian figures while the palaces that flank the grand canal dazzle with byzantine inspired architecture this ambitious exhibit illuminates the lucrative and creative relationship between the italian port city and its islamic neighbors over nine centuries though venice retained its christian identity it enthusiastically embraced islamic ideas and aesthetics as the items on display vividly attest to among them glassworks textiles carpets arms and armor ceramics sculpture paintings metalwork and furniture
