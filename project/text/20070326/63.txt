politics &amp economics chavez plans collective property associated press caracas venezuela president hugo chavez announced his governments sweeping overhauls toward socialism will include the creation of "collective property " vowing to undermine capitalisms influence in venezuela mr chavez said state financed cooperatives would operate under a new concept in which workers would share profits "its property that belongs to everyone and its going to benefit everyone " he said mr chavez said his government fully respects private property but pledged to replace capitalist ideals with socialist principles on cooperatives "it cannot be production to generate profits for one person or a small group of people that become rich exploiting peons who end up becoming slaves living in poverty and misery their entire lives " he said the legal framework for collective property will be established under a forthcoming constitutional reform proposed by mr chavez
