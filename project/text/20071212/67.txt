loan service to halt school emblem ads student financial services inc agreed to stop using college logos and athletic mascots to market its student loan consolidation services as part of a settlement with new york and florida authorities the new york attorney general andrew m cuomo said that by using school emblems in its marketing materials the clearwater fla company confused students into thinking its loans were endorsed by their universities in return for the right to use school colors and logos the company paid schools for each loan application it received the company will end its pacts with colleges and sign a "direct marketing code of conduct " it wont pay a monetary penalty and didnt acknowledge wrongdoing the company declined to comment
