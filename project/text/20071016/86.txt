oao avtovaz joint venture with fiat will produce light cars russian auto maker oao avtovaz yesterday said it signed a memorandum of understanding with italys fiat spa to produce light cars together the agreement covers engineering and technical processes as well as the development production and delivery of engines and other components avtovaz said the russian company said it will form working groups with fiat to determine the conditions for cooperation adding that the companies plan to sign a "concrete agreement" in the next few months the announcement confirms a report in yesterdays edition of the russian business daily vedomosti
