probe of allied capital ends washington federal regulators wrapped up an investigation of allied capital corp without imposing financial penalties or finding that the small business development company had engaged in fraud in connection with how it valued securities in its portfolio the securities and exchange commission said the washington company from mid to early failed to keep records that supported or accurately reflected valuations for some of its securities it also said allied capitals internal controls failed to provide reasonable assurances that it would value the securities in accordance with generally accepted accounting principles the sec ordered allied capital to cease and desist from such violations but didnt accuse the company of fraud or find evidence of financial statement inaccuracies the company settled without admitting or denying wrongdoing
