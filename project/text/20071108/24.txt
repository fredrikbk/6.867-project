china alters rules to guide investment reuters news service beijing china issued new guidelines for foreign direct investment detailing sectors in which it will either promote restrict or ban foreign investment the guidelines issued by the national development and reform commission or ndrc and the commerce ministry will make it more difficult for foreign companies to enter sectors that beijing is trying to rein in including some energy and mining projects by contrast the agencies said they will encourage foreign investment that will help china protect the environment cut pollution and develop renewable energy investment in high technology advanced materials and equipment manufacturing also will be welcome in a statement yesterday on the ndrcs web site the agencies said the new guidelines will help put foreign direct investment to better use to spur innovation promote industrial restructuring and ease regional imbalances priorities set at the recent communist party congress
