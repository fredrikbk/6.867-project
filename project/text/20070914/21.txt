consumer groups protest rising food costs in italy consumer groups organized protests across italy to draw attention to the impact on families of the rising cost of food especially on italians beloved staple pasta activists at protests in rome milan and palermo said the prices of basic commodities are being driven up by middlemen while earnings for farmers remain flat for pasta italians will soon be paying as much as more for their daily serving economists and producers counter that pasta prices are being driven by rising wheat prices world wide the demand for wheat is the result of several trends including an increasing demand for biofuels which can be made from wheat and improved diets in emerging countries where putting meat on the table is raising the demand for feed for livestock says francesco bertolini an economist at milans bocconi university
