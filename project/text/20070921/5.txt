art for moneys sake bijan kherzi makes some salient points in "the new art of art finance" leisure &amp arts sept but he fails to understand two crucial distinctions between art and commerce first damien hirsts "for the love of god" is not art its a form of self promotion second the whole business of buying and selling contemporary works has nothing to do with art or the art market much less the value of art and everything to do with a novel means of moving large sums of money from one investors pocket into another investors pocket alan shuback new york
