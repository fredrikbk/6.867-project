chinas economy grows despite curbs beijing chinas investment growth in august accelerated slightly adding to concerns that beijings tightening measures have been ineffective in keeping economic growth in check chinas urban fixed asset investment in the january to august period rose from the same period last year accelerating slightly from the growth in the first seven months government data showed friday the government doesnt give a monthly growth rate but goldman sachs estimated that investment growth accelerated to in august from in july while the growth rate for the january august period matched economists expectations the latest data follow government figures showing that the country had its fastest consumer price inflation rate since and its second largest trade surplus ever last month heightening concerns about the effectiveness of policy tightening "this round of macroeconomic controls has continued for over four years the relevant agencies have introduced many policy measures but the effects havent been obvious " communist party newspaper the peoples daily reported on friday
