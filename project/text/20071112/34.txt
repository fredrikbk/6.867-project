google inc university start up file patent infringements suit reuters news service northeastern university and a start up company filed a federal patent infringement lawsuit against google inc over technology used in its core web search system according to legal papers filed last week the complaint was filed nov in marshall in the eastern district of texas a court with a history of decisions that are highly favorable to plaintiffs in patent cases the plaintiffs are bostons northeastern university and jarg corp a start up founded by a northeastern university professor who is the licensee of search technology patented in a year before google was incorporated jon murchinson a spokesman for google of mountain view calif said he believed the suit was without merit
