morgan stanley sets co venture morgan stanley and norwegian consulting company det norske veritas launched a venture aimed at tapping into the voluntary carbon market the morgan stanley carbon bank aims to provide companies with voluntary carbon auditing and offsetting voluntary carbon offsets allow companies and individuals to counter carbon dioxide emissions from using a car for instance by buying credits from projects that reduce the amount of co or other greenhouse gases in the atmosphere such as wind farms or tree planting this voluntary market is separate from the obligatory emissions market like the european union emissions trading scheme which sets a carbon price for major eu carbon emitters such as industrial manufacturers and power producers
