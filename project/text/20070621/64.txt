lenders agree to conduct code new york attorney general andrew cuomo said three more lenders have agreed to adopt a code of conduct for student lending mr cuomo said wachovia corp national city corp and regions financial corp have agreed to a code that prohibits financial arrangements between schools and lenders to students bans gifts to college staff members and institutes disclosure standards and restrictions on how preferred lenders are chosen the code of conduct grew out of a probe of the student loan industry by mr cuomos office and has been adopted as law in new york state earlier this week attorneys general in states including mr cuomo wrote a letter to congress urging passage of similar legislation at the national level
