sec revises rule on short selling washington the securities and exchange commission approved a rule change aimed at preventing abusive short selling and market manipulation involving securities offerings the change approved in a vote bars the purchase of shares in an offering by anyone who sold the shares short during a restricted period the sec said the rule will replace a less effective restriction that precludes covering short positions with shares acquired in an initial or secondary offering to ensure the rule doesnt limit the pool of potential buyers for securities offerings the sec included a provision that will give some wiggle room to those who sold shares short in a restricted period leading up to an offering
