wyeth the philippines government accused drug and health care company wyeth based in madison n j of last year removing and destroying batches of infant formula that had rust on the cans without telling the government "wyeth should have notified us when they withdrew the affected goods from the market " said joshua ramos deputy director of the bureau of food and drugs in manila kevin wiggins a wyeth spokesman said there was no reason to inform the philippine agency because the rust was found only on the packaging and was "purely cosmetic " the rust hadnt affected the formula mr wiggins said he confirmed that wyeth had destroyed some cans of the powdered formula and had removed others he said wyeth had alerted the government this spring about the results of an internal investigation and considered the matter closed
