new century financial corp sec investigation escalates into reporting errors new century financial corp said it received a formal order of investigation from the securities and exchange commission regarding errors in its financial reports the order marks an escalation of the secs investigation of the now bankrupt home lender that was at the center of the subprime mortgage crisis this year the irvine calif company said in an sec filing that its outside counsel was advised of the order by sec staff june the company said it was cooperating with the investigation new century financial also disclosed in its sec filing that it was served with a complaint for declaratory judgment by beneficiaries of its executive retirement plan and deferred compensation plan
