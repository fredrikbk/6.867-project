refco trust sues thomas h lee new york a refco inc litigation trust representing creditors of the commodities firm brought down in a scandal have sued the private equity firm that acquired a controlling stake in refco the lawsuit against buyout firm thomas h lee partners lp filed in u s district court in new york alleges lee uncovered red flags about refco before its purchase of the refco stake and didnt follow up because it wanted to complete an initial public offering of refco shares the suit was brought by marc kirschner trustee of refco litigation trust lee denied the claims "while it is understandable that the bankruptcy estate would seek recovery from many parties the allegations against thl are misdirected and entirely without merit " its statement said adding that lee "was one of the victims of a carefully concealed fraud "
