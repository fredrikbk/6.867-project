anglo american plc mondi unit to be spun off as streamlining continues anglo american plc said it expects to spin off its mondi group paper and packaging unit in early july with listings in south africa and london the spinoff is part of efforts to streamline anglo focusing the company on its core mining operations having sold its remaining stake in highveld steel &amp vanadium corp this year it intends to give up its stake in south african anglogold ashanti ltd over the next two years anglo american said that for every anglo shares held investors will receive shares of london listed mondi plc and shares in johannesburg listed mondi ltd anglo american also plans to consolidate existing ordinary shares so investors receive new anglo shares for every shares now held
