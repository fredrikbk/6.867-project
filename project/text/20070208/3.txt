altria group inc plan to spin off kraft unit gets ftc fast track clearance the federal trade commission fast tracked the approval of altria group inc s move to spin off food manufacturing subsidiary kraft foods inc the decision by the competition regulator means the move by altria will receive no further regulatory scrutiny last week tobacco titan altria announced it had decided to completely dispose of its investment in kraft as of march the announcement by the ftc was for the most part a formality as there would have been no competitive reasons to prevent the breakup from occurring as part of the restructuring altria shareholders will get of a kraft share for every altria share they hold altria currently has an stake in kraft the move was broadly welcomed by the market as it will allow kraft to focus on turning around its flagging business and altria to concentrate on tobacco operations
