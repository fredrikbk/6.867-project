baxter international inc indonesia aims to cooperate in creating bird flu vaccine the indonesian government and baxter healthcare corp a unit of baxter international inc signed a memorandum of understanding aimed at future joint cooperation on a human vaccine for hn avian influenza under the memorandum indonesia will provide samples of domestic strains of the hn virus and baxter of deerfield ill will offer technical expertise to produce the vaccine indonesian health minister siti fadilah supari said without providing any financial details a baxter representative indicated that actual cooperation on joint vaccine development remains a long way off the memorandum of understanding "is a framework to continue negotiations its not a contract " said kim bush president of baxter healthcares vaccine unit
