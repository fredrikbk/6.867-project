shadowy presences "radiant darkness the art of nocturnal light" april july the getty center or www getty edu though the impressionists are celebrated for their mastery at capturing daylights elusive quality with the brush it was their old master predecessors th to th centuries who first cast light in a starring role they used light to create drama and to elicit emotional responses from the viewer in narratives that often unfolded at night for them light could carry symbolic meaning divine light signified the presence of god candlelight vanity and faith firelight divine punishment and moonlight mystery this exhibit explores the many ways artists represented nocturnal illumination to imbue the canvas with poetry foreboding mystery and power twenty one works of various media are on display with rembrandt van rijn aert van der neer francesco vanni and gerrit dou among the featured artists
