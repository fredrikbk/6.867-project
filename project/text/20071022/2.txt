airlines skyteam offer of slots draws european commission probe the european commission is looking into an offer by eight airlines in the skyteam alliance to make slots available at european union airports to address antitrust concerns the airlines are aeromexico alitalia spa csa czech airlines delta air lines inc korean air lines co northwest airlines corp and air france and klm both part of air france klm sa they made the offer as part of negotiations during a commission probe into the alliances operations skyteam is suspected of creating barriers to the entry of competitors on routes where it has a strong market position the airlines have offered opening slots to increase competition
