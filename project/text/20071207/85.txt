telstra corp court rules consumers were misled over network telstra corp australias largest telecommunications company misled consumers about the coverage available on its next g mobile network the federal court ruled the melbourne company plans to appeal the decision australias antitrust regulator brought proceedings against telstra saying it deceived consumers by representing the mobile network as having "coverage everywhere you need it " and by claiming next g customers would get the same or better coverage as they did on a code division multiple access network evidence provided by experts showed that coverage was limited by factors such as terrain physical structures and handset selection the regulator said the australian competition and consumer commission will seek injunctions preventing further similar claims
