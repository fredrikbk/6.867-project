toyota recalls pickups washington toyota motor corp said it is recalling four by four versions of the tundra pickup truck in the u s because of problems with a propeller shaft connected to the rear axle a joint in the rear propeller shaft may have been improperly heat treated which could have failed to harden it sufficiently a section of the rear propeller shaft could separate at the joint and immobilize the truck and force it to coast to a stop toyota said no accidents or injuries were linked to the recall
