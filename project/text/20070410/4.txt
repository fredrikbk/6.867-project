bajaj auto ltd motorcycle sales seen struggling in first half indias bajaj auto ltd expects motorcycle sales during the first half of the current financial year to "struggle a bit" due to surplus inventory at its dealers said managing director rajiv bajaj "between april and september we will struggle a bit but in the long term it is looking good " said mr bajaj indias fiscal year ends in march mr bajaj said there was an "inventory buildup" of entry level motorcycles with cubic centimeter engines due to "sluggish" demand in that segment mr bajaj said the entry level segment is being "commoditized" due to intense price wars in that segment between bajaj auto and market leader hero honda motors ltd
