volkswagen ag cars are recalled in u s over headlight standards associated press volkswagen ag said it is recalling passenger cars in the u s because the headlights failed to comply with federal safety standards the recall includes jetta models and rabbit gti and r passenger cars there have been no crashes linked to the issue a company spokesman said some of the head lamps may be missing required caps in the sockets of the low beam horizontal and vertical aiming screws the german auto maker said owners are expected to be notified of the recall beginning later this month
