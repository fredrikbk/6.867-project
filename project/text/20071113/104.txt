publicis venture aims for efficiency starting next year publicis groupe will let clients get media digital and creative services from the same single provider part of an effort to offer marketers more efficiency and boost the sagging fortunes of its leo burnett unit many marketers believe that ad agencies spend too much time fighting with their digital and media partners over how to spend the marketers ad dollars hampering advertisers ability to get the right mix of services publicis will create a new entity the insight factory that will bring together some of the assets and talent from its digital unit digitas its media buying and services firm starcom mediavest and leo burnett which provides the creative services all three of those units will be able to use resources from the joint venture to service their clients the new entity will be overseen by a board of executives made up of agency heads from burnett starcom and digitas
