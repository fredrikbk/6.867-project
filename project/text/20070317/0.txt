low tar ban is world wide washington tobacco manufacturer philip morris usa inc wont be able to sell "light" or "low tar" cigarettes anywhere in the world a federal appeals court judge said in response to a clarification request filed by the altria group inc unit judge gladys kessler said that an august ruling banning "implied health messages" applied to the companys international businesses as well as its domestic operations to rule otherwise would allow tobacco companies "to spread fraudulent and misleading health messages and descriptors about their products throughout the world even though they are prohibited from doing so in the united states " judge kessler said no one from altria or the justice department which brought the suit against the companies could be reached for comment
