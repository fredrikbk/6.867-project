starbucks to open stores in bulgaria associated press starbucks corp said it plans to open coffee houses in bulgaria and portugal next year the first bulgarian store will open in the capital city of sofia and the first store in portugal is expected to open in lisbon the seattle based coffee chain said starbucks will work with joint venture partners in greece and spain that help run the companys coffee houses in several other european countries its bulgaria stores will be operated with athens based marinopoulos group starbucks partner for stores in greece cyprus romania switzerland and austria the expansion into portugal will fall under starbucks partnership with madrid based grupo vips
