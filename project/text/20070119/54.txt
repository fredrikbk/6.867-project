michelin sa tire production is halted at facility in nigeria french tire maker michelin sa said it decided to stop production at its port harcourt plant in nigeria a move a company spokeswoman attributed to the plants difficulties in remaining competitive the spokeswoman said all the plants employees have been temporarily laid off since december michelin and the nigerian government will "work together to find a buyer as soon as possible" for the plant michelin said it would maintain its sales operations and plantations in nigeria port harcourt is the hub of the oil rich niger delta and home base to hundreds of foreign oil workers who have become the target of attacks and kidnappings in recent months the plant is one of two production sites michelin has in africa the companys web site says the other is in algeria
