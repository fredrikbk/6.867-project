british broadcasting corp on demand services approved for launch british broadcasting corp the united kingdom based publicly funded broadcaster was given permission to launch a range of web based on demand services by the bbc trust its regulating and governing body the trust said the new services "are likely to deliver significant public value " but it also warned that some aspects of the plans pose a risk of "potential negative market impact" on commercial media companies the service would allow users to view tv programs from the previous seven days the trust also launched a period of consultation inviting the public the commercial sector and bbc management to contribute on the project
