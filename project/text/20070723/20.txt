deals &amp deal makers hong kong to add closing auction hong kong the hong kong stock exchange plans to introduce a minute auction session to calculate the closing prices of stocks from february the exchanges operator said friday the plan which needs the approval of the securities &amp futures commission will delay the close to p m from p m local time hong kong exchanges &amp clearing ltd said under the proposal stockbrokers will place orders in the minutes between p m and p m the computer system will then execute a "pre order matching" of prices over the last two minutes of that period the closing price will be determined by computerized matching shortly after p m the operator said the plan "will facilitate market participants to complete their clients orders at closing prices and will further align the hong kong securities market with international practices " closing prices are now calculated by taking the middle price of the last five orders before the close
