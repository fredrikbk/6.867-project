automotive brief itochu corp joint venture with suzuki will assemble cars in russia japanese trading company itochu corp said it is planning to build a car assembly plant in russia in cooperation with suzuki motor corp a joint project in russia where demand for cars has been growing rapidly in recent years would extend cooperation between the two japanese companies itochu provides logistics services to suzuki and exports suzuki brand vehicles in several markets including hungary said an itochu spokesman he declined to comment on details of the project including the size of the investment but said information would be released soon a spokesman for suzuki declined to comment
