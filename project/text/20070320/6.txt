automotive brief honda motor co possible engine issues prompt u s auto recall associated press honda motor co said it is recalling about vehicles from the model years in the u s to deal with potential engine problems the japanese auto maker said the recall affects models of the acura rl tl and tsx sedans honda accord coupes sedans and hybrids odyssey minivans and ridgeline trucks the move is part of a global recall because of similar problems said honda spokesman chris martin details on the total number of vehicles and the number of countries where the vehicles were sold werent immediately available he said mr martin said honda discovered that sealer material leaked onto a coil wire inside the fuel pump relay on some of the vehicles the wire can become brittle and snap causing the engine to stall or fail to start honda has received "multiple complaints" of vehicles stalling or failing to start he said there have been no injuries or crashes linked to the defect
