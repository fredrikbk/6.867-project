deutsche boerse ag units go independent under restructuring deutsche boerse ag unveiled long awaited changes to its management structure under which each of the german companys divisions will be operated independently and will have its own representation on the board earlier this month mathias hlubek and matthias ganz resigned as chief financial officer and chief operating officer respectively because they disagreed with the restructuring people familiar with the situation say in the new structure the finance chief will no longer have strategic power while the chief operating officer position will become obsolete after the structure is implemented in the second quarter deutsche boerses board will have a member for each of its business units cash market operations xetra derivatives exchange eurex settlement and custody business clearstream and the information technology operations frank gerstenschlager will become the board member for xetra which has until now been the responsibility of andreas preuss who is also chief executive of eurex
