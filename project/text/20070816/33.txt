glaxosmithkline plc government places order for more avian flu vaccine as part of a government effort to stockpile vaccine in case of a pandemic flu outbreak the department of health and human services has placed an order for additional doses of glaxosmithkline plcs avian flu vaccine glaxo said hhs has placed an order for million doses of its vaccine to prevent the hn flu strain commonly referred to as avian flu the order is in addition to five million doses that the government ordered in november glaxo also said that it has initiated phase i ii clinical trials for its prepandemic vaccine the studies will test the vaccine alone and in combination with one of glaxos adjuvant agents which can boost the vaccines strength results are expected early next year the vaccine ordered will be manufactured at glaxos ste foy canada plant glaxo added that the vaccine for the clinical trials will be produced at its facilities in quebec
