euronext regulators back nyse merger paris the four national stock market regulators that oversee euronext nv approved the pan european stock exchange operators plan to merge with new yorks nyse group inc said the autorite des marches financiers which regulates the paris stock exchange the authorization from the euronext college of regulators is one of several needed for the merger euronext owns stock markets in france the netherlands belgium and portugal as well as the euronext liffe derivatives exchanges in london nyse operates the new york stock exchange euronext is incorporated in the netherlands so the deal needs approval of the dutch government it has been approved already by both firms shareholders the nyse filed its offer document for euronext with the amf this week and is awaiting authorization from the french regulator and the securities and exchange commission the amf should give its decision by thursday
