text of fomc statement online edition the following is the text of the federal reserves decision on interest rates released wednesday mar the federal open market committee decided today to keep its target for the federal funds rate at recent indicators have been mixed and the adjustment in the housing sector is ongoing nevertheless the economy seems likely to continue to expand at a moderate pace over coming quarters recent readings on core inflation have been somewhat elevated although inflation pressures seem likely to moderate over time the high level of resource utilization has the potential to sustain those pressures in these circumstances the committees predominant policy concern remains the risk that inflation will fail to moderate as expected future policy adjustments will depend on the evolution of the outlook for both inflation and economic growth as implied by incoming information voting for the fomc monetary policy action were ben s bernanke chairman timothy f geithner vice chairman thomas m hoenig donald l kohn randall s kroszner cathy e minehan frederic s mishkin michael h moskow william poole and kevin m warsh
