bombardier inc report on crash finds debris in landing gear a preliminary report by danish aviation authorities found there was a piece of debris lodged in the landing gear mechanism of the q aircraft that crashed over the weekend the q turboprop was operated by scandinavian airline sas ab and built by montreal based bombardier inc while the investigation is continuing the agency also noted the finding "is not related" to two previous accidents involving sass q aircraft which also were caused by landing gear failures no one was seriously hurt in any of the incidents the airline grounded its q aircraft this week after saturdays crash the incidents have prompted the european unions aviation safety authority to call a meeting to evaluate the airworthiness of the aircraft
