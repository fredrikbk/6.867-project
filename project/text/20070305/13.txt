chevron corp vote nears on whether to split chairman ceo chevron corp said shareholders will vote at its april annual meeting on a proposal from an investor to amend the company bylaws to separate the roles of chairman and chief executive david oreilly currently holds both titles the san ramon calif oil giant said shareholders also will vote on a proposal requiring chevron to adopt "quantitative goals" in reducing total greenhouse gas emissions according to a preliminary proxy filed with the securities and exchange commission the proposal requires chevron to provide a report to shareholders by sept on its plans to achieve those goals chevron said its board recommended that shareholders vote against both proposals
