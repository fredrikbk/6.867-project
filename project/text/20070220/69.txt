nomura holdings inc watchdog chides unit for actions of employee japans securities and exchange surveillance commission recommended that the financial services agency order nomura securities co to take appropriate steps after an employee sold some investment products without providing sufficient explanation according to a statement from the agency an official at nomuras kyoto branch in western japan gave misleading information to customers about the risks of structured bonds from october through april failing to clearly explain the possibility of the investment products value falling below its principal the financial watchdog said in a statement nomura securities a core subsidiary of nomura holdings inc said in a statement that it "takes the recommendation seriously and will ensure thorough legal compliance to prevent a reoccurrence "
