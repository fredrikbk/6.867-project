changan automobile group chinese firm in a first makes its own hybrid car chinas state owned changan automobile group has started making its own hybrid cars the first such move by a chinese auto maker the official xinhua news agency reported mass production of the chinese designed car which consumes less fuel than ordinary cars of the same size was launched after six years of research and development xinhua said the changan group controls listed changan automobile co a chinese partner of ford motor co and mazda motor corp demand for hybrid cars is negligible in china where gasoline is subsidized and fuel economy generally figures little in purchasing decisions
