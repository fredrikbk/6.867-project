thailand moves to relieve baht bangkok thailand thailands central bank is seeking to relieve upward pressure on the thai currency by introducing ways for local companies to borrow money offshore without affecting the baht exchange rate or running afoul of the countrys capital controls starting thursday thai companies can borrow money overseas if they immediately hedge the proceeds via foreign exchange swaps bank of thailand gov tarisa watanagase said friday this makes it too expensive for speculators to use these loans to bet on the baht firming against other currencies the measure also may encourage local companies to expand the thai economy is flagging after a military coup in september the central bank last month introduced capital controls to fight speculation that had driven the baht to a nine year high on the dollar
