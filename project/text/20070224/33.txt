london tokyo exchanges team up the london stock exchange plc and the tokyo stock exchange inc agreed to enter into an alliance aimed at encouraging access to each others markets and eventually providing round the clock trading the exchanges said the agreement which marks the first time the two exchanges have worked together is a further step in enhancing the pairs international presence as global consolidation and cooperation among financial exchanges continues the lse said a task force of officials from both exchanges will shortly begin regular meetings to consider development opportunities along with encouraging cross border market access for member firms the exchanges intend to create and promote jointly traded products and discuss the operations and regulation of markets for growth companies
